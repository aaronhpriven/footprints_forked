What is a Footprint?

A footprint is evidence of a book copy at a moment in time and space, e.g. an auction catalog listing the sale of a book.


How is a Footprint Recorded?

At the most basic, a footprint is comprised of a book title and a description of the evidence. More information about the footprint and written work is added after these pieces of information are gathered.
